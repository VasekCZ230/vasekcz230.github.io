MyBeta v1.2.4

MyBeta is my own MultiMC modpack for Minecraft Beta 1.7.3, which contains a selection of mods that enhance the classic beta experience. With mods like How Many Items, BetaTweaks, SinglePlayerCommands, Rei's Minimap, World Edit and more.

MyPVP v2.3.0.1

MyPVP is my own MultiMC modpack for Minecraft 1.8.9, designed to enhance the PvP experience with a variety of performance and utility mods. It includes ArmorStatusHUD, BetterFPS, Canele x3 Keystrokes, Combo Display, Chunk Animator, MemoryFix, Old Animations Mod, OptiFine, Powns Coords HUD, Status Effect HUD, Vanilla Enhancements, Xaero's Minimap, and more to improve performance, and overall gameplay for a smoother and better PvP experience.

Rainbow Aero Cursor

This is my own custom remake of the original Rainbow Cursor from Windows XP, redesigned to match the Aero style of Windows 7. It flashes a vibrant rainbow effect every few seconds.

3D Maze Screensaver

The 3D Maze screensaver is a classic screensaver that was included with Windows 95 and Windows 98. It was one of several screensavers provided to prevent screen burn-in on CRT monitors, and it became a nostalgic piece of computer history for many users.
