Mindustry

Mindustry is a free, open-source tower defense and factory management game developed by Anuke. It combines elements from real-time strategy (RTS) games and resource management, making it stand out for its blend of both factory-building and defense mechanics. Originally released as a mobile game, it has since expanded to multiple platforms, including PC and console, and enjoys a dedicated community due to its depth and modding support.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox adventure game where players explore a block-based world, gather resources, craft tools, and build anything they can imagine. The game features multiple modes, including Survival, Creative, and Hardcore, offering different ways to play.

Old School RuneScape

Old School RuneScape is a classic fantasy MMORPG based on the 2007 version of RuneScape. Players explore an open world filled with quests, dungeons, and unique regions while leveling up dozens of skills. The game features a player-driven economy, challenging bosses, and both PvE and PvP activities. Its nostalgic graphics and regular community-voted updates keep it popular among fans who enjoy a traditional, skill-based online adventure.
